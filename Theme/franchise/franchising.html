Franchising in Anime

Anime franchises often grow to be quite large, with various games, mangas, and/or light novels within one franchise. An anime is rarely the source for a franchise--most animes are adapted from mangas, the Japanese term for what we would consider comic books. Some of the most notable franchises include:

  • Dragon Ball
  • Pokemon (though whether or not this is an anime is debated upon by otakus and weebs alike)
  • Naruto
  • One Piece
  • Gundam
  • Fate/

...and many more. Notably, a large percentage current successdul franchises are from shonen anime.
