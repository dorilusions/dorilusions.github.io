The Genres of Anime That You Need to Know

Action/Shonen

The most "mainstream" kind of anime, and is an action-packed show about character bonds and fighting. Also, includes lotf of tournament arcs and fighting, as well as superpowers and fighting. Did I mention fighting?

Romance/Shojo

These solely exist to make you jealous and fluffy on the inside at the same time, however that works.

Horror

This is hands-down the best genre, if you're dead on the inside like I am and completely desensitized to gore. Somehow, this genre is often put together with another genre named Ecchi, which I won't go into detail for the purity and innosence of the world.

Slice of Life

These are just feel-good, high school girl everyday life playing instruments and chasing aliens kind of shows.
