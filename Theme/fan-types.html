Types of Anime Fans

The Ones Who Have A Life/The Casual

These sorts of anime fans have somewhat prioritized and put anime in the backseat, but still enjoy the occasional show if they have time. The healthiest type of anime fan.

The Collector

This sort of anime fan spends large amounts of money on anime merchandise they can't afford. Comes hand in hand with college debt.

The Hype Beast

Watches anime soley for the excitement and not for the plot, which is highly annoying for the Intellectuals.

The Intellectual

The Intellectual feels that they have the greatest taste in anime, and finds the Hype Beast to have extremely trash taste. Likes to use the word "plot".
