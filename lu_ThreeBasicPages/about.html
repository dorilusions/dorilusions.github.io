Some Things About Me You're Probably Not Interested In Knowing

This beautiful, impeccable, flawless being on the right is me. If I look nothing like my canvas profile picture, that's because my Canvas profile was the result of me surpressing my natrual look while standing in the cold for my high school senior picture. I think I definitely look better in this picture -->.

Why am I holding sticks, you might be asking? That's because I'm in a club called Taiko Kai at the University of Washington, which is a form of traditional Japanese drumming. Despite my obsession with all things Japanese, I'm 100% (or so I've been told by my parents) American-born Chinese. Taiko is the one club in my life I've truly found enjoyable, though I might suffer hearing loss before I turn 20 as a result.
