Some Recommendations by Dorothy

Shonen/Action: One Piece

The complexity of the plot goes off the charts. Combined with the lovable, quirky characters, its a must watch. Only problem: its over 800 episodes long.....

Shonen/Romance: Akatsuki no Yona

Probably the best character developemt I've ever seen in a show, plus has the cutest romantic plotline as well.

Shoujo/Romance/Tragedy: Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso/Your Lie in April

Prepare your tissues, you're going on one heck of a ride....

Horror: Death Parade

This really makes you think about the meaning of life, and also what awaits you after death.

Isekai: No Game No Life

My favorite anime of all time. Its satisfying, its funny, and ITS ART IS SO FREAKING AMAZING.
